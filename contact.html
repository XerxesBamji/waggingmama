Quick Answers

Common Questions

How quickly will you reply?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours, usually much sooner. For urgent enquiries please call us directly.

Is the meet & greet really free?

Absolutely! We never charge for the initial meet & greet. It's important we all feel happy and comfortable before starting.

What areas do you cover?

We cover Barry and the Vale of Glamorgan. Drop us a message with your postcode and we'll confirm if we can help.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer or cash. Payment is typically due at the end of each week for regular clients, or in advance for boarding stays.
