About Wagging Mama

We're dog lovers, first and always. Our mission is to give every dog the care, love, and adventure they deserve.

Meet Us

Passionate About
Every Pup We Meet

Wagging Mama was born out of a simple love for dogs and a desire to offer something more personal than a traditional kennels. We believe dogs are family, and they deserve to be treated that way — even when you can't be there.

Based locally, we offer dog walking and home boarding services that prioritise your dog's comfort, safety, and happiness above everything else. Every dog in our care is given individual attention, cuddles, and plenty of tail-wagging fun.

We are fully insured, pet first aid trained, and DBS checked — because your peace of mind matters just as much as your dog's wellbeing.
