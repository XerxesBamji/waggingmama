Got Questions?

Boarding FAQs

Does my dog need to be vaccinated? ▼

Yes, all dogs must be up-to-date with their core vaccinations (including Kennel Cough) to stay with us. Please bring your dog's vaccination records at the meet & greet.

How many dogs board at once? ▼

We keep boarding numbers intentionally small – a maximum of 1–2 dogs at any one time, depending on size and compatibility. This ensures personalised attention for every dog.

Can I bring my dog's own bed and toys? ▼

Absolutely! We actively encourage it. Familiar scents and favourite toys help dogs settle in quickly and feel at home.

What are your drop-off and pick-up times? ▼

Drop-off is usually between 8am–10am and collection between 4pm–6pm. We're flexible where possible – just let us know your situation and we'll do our best to accommodate.
